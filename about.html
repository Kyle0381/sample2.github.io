About us

We started as a group of players who's looking to help newbies and other veteran players that needs help in accomplishing daily and weekly tasks in game. We noticed that some of these players are too busy in real life to the point that they do not have time to play the game anymore. An idea came to us that we can help those players make progress on the game by providing our service in return of money. As much as we would like to continue doing those things for free, unfortunately, we cannot as we also invest time in helping them.

Mission

Ragnarok 2.0 aims to provide ease of accomplishing daily and weekly tasks for new and veteran players as well as providing a friendlier and ejoyable experience for all Ragnarok players in Valhalla server especially the new ones.


Vision

Our vision is to create a better gaming experience for Ragnarok players in Valhalla server.
